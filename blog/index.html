Music is uncountable

Our project aims at highlighting songs spreading pattern in the world. Using Spotify's weekly ranking in 53 countries, we are able to show popularity timelines of songs around the world.

  • Get Started

Focus on a song

Our first objective was to create a visualization focusing on a song, highlighting how its popularity spread around the world and how it faded.

We imagined displaying a histogram of weekly streams for the song, showing how its popularity evolved through the year.

To highlight the popularity according to the region we had the idea to display a world map with circles on the countries, with area varying according to the number of weekly streams, and inside the circle the ranking of the song in the country's chart.

First version

The first thing we did was to upload a map of the world. Then we added a research bar to allow the user to look for a song and select it. When the music is selected, the total number of streamings in each country is calculated and the information displays when hovering a country.

First version

When the user hover a country, the total number of streaming for the chosen music is displayed.

First version

The user could also click on a country in the map, which triggers a zoom and let a window appear, suggesting to click a on a button to display two rankings (one displaying the best songs and the other displaying the best artists in the country selected).

2-pages design

When focusing on a song, we wanted also to provide insight about its popularity. We decided to sort the countries by weekly streams and display a ranking.

We designed a visualization in two pages with:

  • The worldmap on top
  • The countries ranking on the bottom
This design aimed at allowing the user to switch from one page ton another easily by scrolling or clicking on a button.

A menu on the right would remains on screen at all time allowing the user to change the song to display.

Focus on a country

Appart from focusing on song, we also wanted to offer the possibility to highlight a specific country's music tastes, varying with time.

The idea is to zoom on the user's selected country on the map, and display image or titles of the most popular songs, with area varying according to the songs popularity. A timeline is also used to display the ranking according to the time of the year.

On the rank page, we would sort according to the number of streams in the selected country and display them, with their informations.

A menu on the right would remains on screen at all time allowing the user to change the country to display, or swith to song mode and vice-versa.
